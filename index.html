About Me

Kim holds an undergraduate degree from the University of Glasgow, BEng Aerospace Systems, where she graduated as the best in her class. She then joined the University of Strathclyde, where she completed a year of MRes training in offshore renewable energy. During her years at the University of Strathclyde, she worked as a researcher with Siemens Gamesa Renewable Energy. She has also worked on projects with Natural Power and RES. She is passionate about teaching STEM and has been volunteering with the Engineering Development Trust Scotland, helping increase the exposure of young people to STEM, and has been nominated for the volunteer of the year.
